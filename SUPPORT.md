# Getting Help

## Project-specific questions

For questions about a specific project:
1. Check the project's documentation and README
2. Search [Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/microconsortium/discussions)
3. Open a discussion if your question isn't answered

## Bugs and feature requests

Use the issue tracker in the relevant project repository.

## Other inquiries

- General: info@microconsortium.com
- Security: security@microconsortium.com
- Press/media: press@microconsortium.com

We aim to respond within 5 business days, but as a volunteer-driven project,
response times may vary.
